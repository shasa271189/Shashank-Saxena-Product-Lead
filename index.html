About Me

A decade-plus journey in product management, from startups to enterprise giants

I'm Shashank Saxena, a Lead Product Manager currently at Lowe's India, where I've pioneered rapid AI prototyping practices and led transformative initiatives in ad-tech monetization.

My career spans building enterprise-grade B2B/B2C platforms for organizations like Lowe's, Airtel, Shaadi.com, Knowlarity, and Quikr. I specialize in ad-tech monetization, search ads, and AI-driven product discovery.

At Lowe's, I've scaled the Sponsored Product Ads program from zero to one state, targeting a $100M+ ad margin impact. I introduced rapid prototyping using Windsurf, scaling our practice from 2 to 9 PM team members.

I'm an IIT Guwahati graduate with deep technical expertise in Python, SQL, AWS, and GenAI tools. I believe in data-driven decision making, rapid prototyping, and building products that create genuine value for users.
